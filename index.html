About Me


What can I say about me? Let's just say that I am hard to forget. I am a fun, and outgoing person. I love a challenge. I am always learning something new. I love photography and music.

A little about my background: I was born in Ukraine. I came to America when I was 5 years old, and grew up in Portland, OR. When I was 18, I moved to Charlotte, NC and have lived here since then. I love living here. It is a beautiful, fast-paced, growing city.

I am really excited about this course. So far, it has been a challenge, but in a good way. All good things take time to develop. But I know that I enjoy it, not only from my inner excitement, but because I do not dread waking up early just to come to class!!
